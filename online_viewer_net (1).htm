About Falcon Cooling Pads

Superior Cooling Performance

Falcon International's evaporative cooling pads are engineered using premium quality cellulose material that provides exceptional cooling efficiency while maintaining optimal airflow.

Our pads feature a specially designed cross-fluted configuration that maximizes the surface area for evaporation, resulting in cooler air output and lower energy consumption compared to conventional cooling pads.

The advanced manufacturing process ensures uniform water distribution across the entire pad surface, preventing dry spots and maintaining consistent cooling performance throughout the system's operation.

Key Features

Excellent Water Absorption

Specially treated cellulose fibers provide superior water retention and distribution across the entire pad surface.

Optimal Airflow

Precision-engineered flute design ensures maximum air contact while minimizing pressure drop.

Durable Construction

Rigid frame support and anti-rot additives extend pad life even in harsh operating conditions.

Eco-Friendly

Made from recyclable materials and helps reduce energy consumption compared to conventional AC systems.

Anti-Microbial

Special treatment inhibits algae and bacterial growth, maintaining hygienic air output.

Easy Maintenance

Simple to clean and replace, minimizing downtime and maintenance costs.
